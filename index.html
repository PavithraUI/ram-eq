Our Core Beliefs

We believe that Execution Capability is a living, breathing and critical aspect of an organization.

Why do good ideas often fail? After all we are a nation of great thinkers and the average quality of mind of the Indian manager is way ahead is way ahead of most others. Ask any Indian manager to come up with a strategy and chances are you'll see an output of high quality. So what's the problem? In a word, Execution.

Our purpose

We at ExQ have a strong and unshakeable view that most of the time we, as individuals, as teams and as organizations, do an adequate to a superb job in strategizing the business. However, most organizations, most of the time, fail in executing against their strategy. And then, the ideas are blamed for the failures, a fresh set of ideas are generated and the circle of corporate life goes on...

On the flip slide lies the immense opportunity - any comapny that can out-execute its competitors has a massive, sustainable advantage. Our purpose is to work with companies to bring awareness on this most seriuos problem more...
